How to Access Services

  1. Select your desired healthcare service
  2. Complete the application form
  3. Provide required medical documents
  4. Submit and track your application

Required Documents

  • National ID or Passport
  • Medical Insurance Card (if applicable)
  • Previous Medical Records
  • Doctor's Referral (if required)

Processing Time

  • Health Insurance: 3-5 business days
  • Medical Appointments: 1-2 business days
  • Medical Devices: 5-7 business days
  • Prescription Services: 1 business day
